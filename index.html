Welcome to Agarwal Group

Agarwal Coal Resources LLP is a trusted name in the coal trading industry in India, dealing in high-quality imported and Indian coal. Based in Amravati, Maharashtra, with branches in Chandrapur and Nagpur, we’ve been driving industrial energy needs for over 55 years.

We partner with mines across SECL, WCL, and MCL, and import coal from Indonesia, Tanzania and South Africa, ensuring consistent supply to industries like power, cement, and manufacturing.

About Us

With over five decades of industry leadership, Agarwal Coal Resources LLP has built a robust distribution network and earned the trust of clients across sectors. Our coal is carefully sourced, quality-tested, and delivered on time — helping power plants and factories function smoothly.

We blend tradition with technology, focusing on operational efficiency, sustainability, and excellent customer service.
